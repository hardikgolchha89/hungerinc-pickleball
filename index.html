How We Play
Rules
🏸

Badminton-Style Scoring on a Pickleball Court

Every rally wins a point — no side-out scoring. Group stage is first to 15 (fast and fun). Knockout is first to 21 (higher stakes). Win by 2. All standard pickleball court rules apply.

🔢

Scoring

  • Rally point — every rally earns a point
  • Group stage: first to 15
  • Knockout: first to 21
  • Must win by 2 clear points
  • Single game per group stage match
🏓

Serving

  • Underhand serve, paddle below wrist
  • Hit ball below the waist
  • Serve diagonally into opposite box
  • Must clear the kitchen (NVZ)
  • One serve attempt — no lets
🍳

The Kitchen Rule

  • Non-Volley Zone = 7 ft either side of net
  • No volleying while in the kitchen
  • May enter the kitchen to play a bounced ball
  • Momentum into kitchen after volley = fault
⬇️

Double Bounce Rule

  • Return of serve must bounce before being hit
  • Serving team must also let the return bounce
  • After that, both teams may volley freely
🚫

Faults

  • Ball lands out of bounds
  • Ball hits the net
  • Volleying in the kitchen
  • Breaking the double bounce rule
  • Player or paddle touches the net
🤝

Spirit of the Game

  • Call your own faults honestly
  • When in doubt — replay the rally
  • Experienced players: help your team!
  • Everyone's here to have fun 🥒
